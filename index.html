Hello! I'm Bryan (Félix), and this is my personal collection of notes, writings, and projects. I am currently a Data Scientist for Hennepin County in Minnesota. My work involves applying statistical and machine learning methods to public domain datasets. I also contribute to the development of data-related software for mainstream use.

I earned my doctorate in mathematics from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities. My dissertation explored applications of modern mathematics to biological systems modeling, particularly using topological arguments to analyze complex dynamical systems. My dissertation also included work on sensitivity analysis, numerical methods for partial differential equations (PDEs), and machine learning techniques for optimization.
